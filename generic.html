Let's Make Something Extraordinary.

Hi there! I'm a fourth-year student at Duke University, majoring in Computer Science & Linguistics, with a double major in Classical Languages and a minor in Classical Archaeology. My academic journey is marked by a 3.95 GPA and leadership roles such as President of Duke A Cappella Council and Business Manager for the Pitchforks of Duke University.

I'm currently interning at Disney Streaming, soaking up all things Product Management in sunny Santa Monica, California, where I'm joining a team to tackle Account Sharing.

Before this gig, I started my work at Out in Tech, making sure events for our awesome community of 50,000 members kick off without a hitch. Back at Duke University, I'm diving into the world of software development, building cool projects for the Innovation Co-Lab and lending a hand as a student developer.

When I'm not knee-deep in code, you might find me leading discussions on data structures and algorithms as an undergraduate teaching assistant, building courses for students to learn how to bring their project idea into a reality, or managing the business side of things for the Pitchforks of Duke University.

Outside of campus life, I've been consulting and teaching high schoolers with Kyros AI, unleashing their potential one project at a time. I've also had the chance to explore ancient cities in Italy as a field archaeologist, unearth some cool artifacts, and even showcase our work in Milan.

Oh, and did I mention I make a mean cup of coffee? From digging in the dirt to diving into databases, I'm all about exploring new horizons. As I look forward to graduation in Spring 2025, I'm excited to see where my journey takes me next.
